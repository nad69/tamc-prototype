Register for a tax reduction if you're married
or in a civil partnership

Most people can earn up to £10,500 a year before paying tax on their earnings. This is called a Personal Allowance. If you earn less than £10,500, you can share your unused Personal Allowance with your partner to reduce the amount of tax they pay.

Eligible couples could get up to£210back each year

This service will be available on 6 April 2015 but you can tell us if you're interested now to receive an email or text reminder to apply later. Registering takes around 5 minutes.

Before you start

An eligible couple must:

  • be married or in a civil partnership
  • both be born after 1935
  • both earn less than £42,285 each
  • have only one person earning less than £10,500

You don't need to be a British citizen or have been living in the UK for a minimum amount of time to apply.
