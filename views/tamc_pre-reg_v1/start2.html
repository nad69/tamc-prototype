Apply for a tax reduction if you're married
or in a civil partnership

If you're married or in a civil partnership you may be able to reduce the tax you pay.

Most people can earn up to £10,500 a year before they pay tax. If you earn less than this,
you can share your tax free allowance with your partner.

Eligible couples could get up to £210 back each year.

An eligible couple must:

  • be married or in a civil partnership
  • both be born after 1935

This service will be available in 2015 but you can tell us if you're interested now.
Registering takes around 5 minutes.

Before you start

You will need to know:

  • your income
  • your partner's income
