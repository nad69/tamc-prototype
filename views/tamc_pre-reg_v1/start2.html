Register your interest in a new tax reduction
for married couples and civil partnerships

Most people can earn up to £10,500 a year before paying tax on their income. This is called a Personal Allowance.

From 6th April 2015, if your income is less than £10,500, you can share your unused Personal Allowance with your partner to reduce the amount of tax they pay.

Couples could benefit from an annual tax reduction of up to £210

An eligible couple must:

  • be married or in a legally registered civil partnership
  • be born after 1935
  • be living in the UK

Find out if you or your partner can share your unused personal allowance and if eligible, register your interest.
This will only take around 5 minutes.

Before you start

You'll need to know:

  • your income
  • your partner's income
