Am I eligible?

You are eligible to share your personal tax allowance with your partner or spouse if:

  • you are married or in a legally registered civil partnership
  • you have an annual income of less than £10,600 per year
    (that's around £883.33 per month or £220.83 per week)
  • your partner or spouse has an annual income of more than £10,600 but less than £42,285 per year
  • you were both born after 1935
  • you both live in the UK
