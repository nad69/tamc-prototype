Am I eligible?

You are eligible to transfer £1060 of your personal allowance to your partner or spouse if:

  • you are married or in a legally registered civil partnership
  • you have an annual income of less than £10,600 per year (around £880 per month)
  • your partner or spouse has an annual income of more than £10,600 but less than £42,285 per year
  • you were both born on or after 6 April 1935
