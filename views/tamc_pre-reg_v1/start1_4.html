Marriage Allowance

Couples may benefit from an annual tax reduction of up to£212

Most people can earn up to £10,600 a year before paying tax on their income which includes pensions, savings and investments. This is called a Personal Allowance.

For the 2015/2016 tax year, if your income is less than £10,600 (£880 a month), you can transfer £1060 of your Personal Allowance to your partner. This may reduce the amount of tax they pay.

Find out if you're eligible and register your interest. This will only take 2-3 minutes.
