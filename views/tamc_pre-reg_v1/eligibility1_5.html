Am I eligible?

You could transfer £1060 of your Personal Allowance to your spouse or civil partner if:

  • you are married or in a legally registered civil partnership
  • you have an annual income of less than £10,600 a year (around £880 a month)
  • your spouse or civil partner has an annual income of more than £10,600 but less than £42,285 a year
  • you were both born on or after 6 April 1935
