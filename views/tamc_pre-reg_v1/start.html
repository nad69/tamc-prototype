Apply for a tax reduction if you're married
or in a civil partnership

If you're married or in a civil partnership you may be able to reduce the tax your partner pays.

Most people can earn up to £10,500 a year before they pay tax. If you earn less than this, you can share your tax free allowance with your partner.

Eligible couples could get up to £210 back each year.

This service will be available on 6 April 2015 but you can tell us if you're interested now. You will receive an email or text nearer when this service is available. Registering takes around 5 minutes.

Before you start

An eligible couple must:

  • be married or in a civil partnership
  • both be born after 1935
  • both earn less than £42,285 each
  • have one person earning less than £10,500
