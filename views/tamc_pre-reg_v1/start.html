Apply for a tax reduction if you're married
or in a civil partnership

If you're married or in a civil partnership you may be able to reduce the tax you pay.

Most people can earn up to £10,000 a year before they pay tax. If you earn less than this, you can share your tax free allowance with your partner.

Eligible couples could get up to £200 back each year.

This service will be available in 2015 but you can tell us if you're interested now. Registering takes around 5 minutes.

Before you start

An eligible couple must:

  • be married or in a civil partnership (same sex)
  • both be born after 1935

Transferrable allowances for married couples and civil partners
