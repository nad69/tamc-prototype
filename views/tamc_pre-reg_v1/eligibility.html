Am I eligible?

You are eligible to share your personal tax allowance with your partner or spouse if:

  • you are married or in a legally registered civil partnership
  • you earn less than £10,500 per year
  • your partner or spouse earns more than £10,500 but less than £42,285 per year
  • you were both born after 1935
  • you both live in the UK
