digidentity

Your digital identity in 5 steps

1
Create profile
Your digital identity begins by creating a Digidentity profile. With this identity you will be able to securely access online services provided by companies or government agencies.
2
Email confirmation
It is important that you add an email address to your profile. This can help you regain access to your profile in the case that you forget your password.
3
Secure your profile
To protect your digital identity we advise that you add 2-step login to your profile. This can be done, for example, by having a confirmation code sent to you via sms each time you log in.
4
Add personal details
We will ask for some personal details like your name or address to verify your identity. The specific questions asked of you will vary depending on the service you wish to access.
5
Prove your identity
To assure that you are who you say you are, we need proof of your identity, such as a passport or driving licence. Some service providers require a copy while others only need partial data.
Registration complete
After we verified your details, your Digidentity profile is ready to use and you can login to one of the service providers.

easy

Verify your identity with ease

Prove your identity through our manual procedure or with our free mobile app, available for iOS and Android.

With our mobile app you can use your smartphone to scan and upload your identity documents. We can then directly process your personal data and confirm that you are who you say you are. This creates a hassle-free registration experience, verifying your identity in the fastest way possible.

Create a safe, digital identity

Everyone should be able to identify themselves online in a simple, secure way. That is our mission! Digidentity offers solutions that enable safe digital communications between individuals, companies and governmental agencies. To this end, we are continuously developing new and improved services. security

Security and service as part of our process

At Digidentity, security is taken more than seriously. Data protection and privacy are of paramount importance to us. This means we start by providing a safe environment. Only when the system is completely secure will design be implemented. Thus, safety is guaranteed.

We consider good service to be as important as infallible security. You can rely on our work to be meticulous and service-minded, and that we are readily available via chat, email and over the telephone during business hours. Any complaints or problems will be solved swiftly, and will be registered so that we can learn from them and make improvements to our service.
