Before you start

You’ll be able to apply for Marriage Allowance if you meet the following criteria:

  • you’re married or civil partnership
  • you have an annual income of £10,600 or less - including pensions, savings and investments
  • your spouse or civil partner has an annual income of between £10,601 and £42,385
  • you were both born on or after 6 April 1935

You’ll need the following information:

  • your spouse or civil partner’s first and last name
  • their gender
  • their National Insurance (NI) number

You’ll also need access to your own information. The GOV.UK Verify account will take you through this, but you must verify your identity to use this service for the first time.

You’ll need:

  • •a valid UK passport or UK photocard driving licence
  • access to your financial information
  • your spouse or civil partner has an annual income of between £10,601 and £42,385
  • you were both born on or after 6 April 1935

You only have to verify your identity once - after that you can sign in with your username and password.

Applying for Marriage Allowance takes about 10 minutes to apply. You’ll get an email confirmation.
