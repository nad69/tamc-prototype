HM Revenue & Customs

 

You recently registered your interest in Marriage Allowance and are now invited to apply. Follow your personal link below.

As a couple you may benefit by up to £212 a year.

To apply online:

  • you, as the person transferring part of your personal allowance, will need to verify your identity at GOV.UK Verify
  • you'll need both your own and your spouse or civil partner's National Insurance (NI) number. You can find this on your National Insurance card if you have one or in official paperwork such as payslips or letters about benefits or tax credits

You can read more about identity verification at GOV.UK/verify. It may take you up to 20 minutes if you are verifying your identity for the first time.
